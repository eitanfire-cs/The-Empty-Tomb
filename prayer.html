What is Prayer?


Philippians 4:6-7 NIV
"Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God. And the peace of God, which transcends all understanding, will guard your hearts and your minds in Christ Jesus."

Prayer is our direct conversations with God. We go to prayer not just to present our requests but also to give thanks, be in God's presence, pray for forgiveness, pray for others, and pray for God's will. When you pray to God, he isn't looking for a script or fancy words, he wants you, your heart, he wants where your at not your performance. Give God your requests not to make him aware of your needs, but to surrender them to him and trust that he is always listening like he says and will help us in his will, which is the best thing for us. We can pray at all times, in every situation, standing, sitting, in the morning, at night, in the office, on the bus, anytime, anywhere, anyway, God is always listening, welcoming you to come into his arms ready to give you love.
