What is the Bible?


2 Timothy 3:16-17
"All Scripture is God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness, so that the servant of God may be thoroughly equipped for every good work."

The Bible is God's living and active word. His word helps guide us in knowing who our God is, discerning the truth, and gives us a roadmap to life of what God's will is and how we should live to live our best life. The Bible helps defend us against spiritual warfare and from falling into the lies and evil of this broken world. It is God's love letter to us.
