What is Worship?


Psalm 29:2
"Ascribe to the Lord the glory due his name; worship the Lord in the splendor of holiness."

Worship is not just about singing songs, its devoting your life to Christ, glorifying him in everything you do. You can do it at the grocery store, being friendly to the clerks, or at the basketball court, helping teamates up and encouraging others. You glorify God with how you live your life, with love. Worship is being in awe of our God, what hes done, and how good he is. You can also use the gifts and talents he gave you to glorify him, whether thats art, coding, sports or cooking!
